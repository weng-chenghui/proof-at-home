The creation pipeline

From curiosity to curriculum in six steps.

🧭

Pick a topic

Browse existing series or choose any domain — finance, physics, music theory, anything.

pah series list
✎

Write conjectures

Formalize statements as conjectures in Lean or Rocq. Import existing ones or define your own.

pah conjecture list
⚡

Prove them

AI-assisted, pair with coding assistant, or manual. The compiler verifies all paths equally.

pah contribution run
📖

Build lessons

Turn proofs into structured educational content. AI helps generate explanations and annotations.

pah lesson create --topic <t>
📚

Compose series

Package related lessons into a course series with a learning path.

pah series create --lessons ...
✨

AI annotations

The AI tutor sidebar annotates lessons with context, suggested questions, and interactive hints.

Everything is verified. Everything is credited.

Every proof is checked by the Lean or Rocq compiler. Your contributions are permanently recorded with NFT metadata — a verifiable record of who created what, when, and how.

What the NFT means

The NFT is not about ownership — content is public domain (CC0). The NFT is a permanent receipt: "This was formalized because of your work."

Think of it like a donor's name on a university building. The building is public. The name records who made it possible. Your NFT records your contribution, your proof method, and a cryptographic signature — pinned to IPFS, mintable on-chain.
